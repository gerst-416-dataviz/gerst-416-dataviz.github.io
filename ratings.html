Let's take a look at how IMDb users rate movies over time...

Pictured on the left is a scatterplot of average user rating versus release year for all movies listed on IMDb with at least 1,000 user ratings. Movies with more ratings are given larger circles on a logarithmic scale, with the largest indicating the maximum of roughly 3,000,000 user ratings.

There are over 40,000 movies that fit that criterion!
